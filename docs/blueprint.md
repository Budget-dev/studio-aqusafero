# **App Name**: AquaSafe Hub

## Core Features:

- Service Catalog: Browse and view detailed information on available RO water treatment services, including specifications and application areas.
- Inquiry & Quote Form: A contact form allowing users to submit specific service inquiries or request a personalized quote for their needs.
- About Us & Trust Signals: Dedicated pages detailing company information, expertise, client testimonials, and case studies to build trust.
- RO Solution Recommendation Tool: An AI-powered tool that suggests suitable RO water treatment solutions based on user inputs such as industry type, required water quality, and daily capacity.
- Article & Resource Library: A section providing informative articles and resources about water treatment, RO technology, and industry best practices.
- Basic Admin Service Management: An internal interface for managing and updating service descriptions, pricing (if applicable), and other public-facing content.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional royal blue (#24488F), evoking trust and the 'Aqua Safe' brand identity. This color contrasts effectively with the light background.
- Background color: A very light blue-grey (#F0F2F4), providing a clean, fresh canvas that complements the primary blue, inspired by water and purity.
- Accent color: A dark, muted blue-green (#334346), selected for its subtle contrast and complementary harmony with the primary blue, while avoiding vibrant teal tones. It helps highlight key interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), for a modern, technical, and precise feel, reflecting advanced water treatment technology.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), paired with 'Space Grotesk' for excellent readability and a neutral, professional appearance in longer text blocks.
- Use a set of clean, line-based icons that visually represent water treatment processes, safety, and industrial applications, maintaining the brand's aesthetic of clarity and reliability.
- A modern, responsive, and organized layout emphasizing clarity and ease of navigation. Service information will be presented in digestible sections with clear call-to-actions, echoing the structured efficiency seen in advanced water treatment plants.
- Implement subtle, smooth micro-animations for navigation transitions, form interactions, and card hover states to enhance user engagement without distracting from the informative content.
